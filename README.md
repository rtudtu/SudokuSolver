# SudokuSolver
Sudoku Solver  
Developed by: Richard Tu  
Made in: June 2017  
A solver that will take in as input either a 4x4, 5x5, or 9x9 sudoku board and return a fully solved board. Some sudoku boards have multiple solutions. This solver will return the first solver 

For Example:
Board: 
//===================================\\
|| 5 | - | 8 | - | 7 | 3 | 1 | 9 | - ||
|| 9 | - | - | 6 | - | - | 4 | - | 8 ||
|| - | - | - | 9 | - | 8 | - | 3 | 5 ||
||-----------+-----------+-----------||
|| - | 7 | - | - | - | - | - | 6 | - ||
|| - | - | 2 | - | - | - | 9 | - | - ||
|| - | 1 | - | - | - | - | - | 8 | - ||
||-----------+-----------+-----------||
|| 1 | 9 | - | 3 | - | 6 | - | - | - ||
|| 2 | - | 3 | - | - | 7 | - | - | 9 ||
|| - | 8 | 7 | 1 | 9 | - | 3 | - | 4 ||
\\===================================//

Final Board: 
//===================================\\
|| 5 | 2 | 8 | 4 | 7 | 3 | 1 | 9 | 6 ||
|| 9 | 3 | 1 | 6 | 2 | 5 | 4 | 7 | 8 ||
|| 7 | 6 | 4 | 9 | 1 | 8 | 2 | 3 | 5 ||
||-----------+-----------+-----------||
|| 3 | 7 | 9 | 2 | 8 | 4 | 5 | 6 | 1 ||
|| 8 | 5 | 2 | 7 | 6 | 1 | 9 | 4 | 3 ||
|| 4 | 1 | 6 | 5 | 3 | 9 | 7 | 8 | 2 ||
||-----------+-----------+-----------||
|| 1 | 9 | 5 | 3 | 4 | 6 | 8 | 2 | 7 ||
|| 2 | 4 | 3 | 8 | 5 | 7 | 6 | 1 | 9 ||
|| 6 | 8 | 7 | 1 | 9 | 2 | 3 | 5 | 4 ||
\\===================================//
